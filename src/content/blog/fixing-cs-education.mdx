Why we need more systems programming and less framework fatigue. A critique of the modern curriculum and a proposal for a more "bottom-up" approach to learning computer science.

## the problem

Most CS programs today focus heavily on high-level abstractions. Students learn React before they understand how the DOM works. They use ORMs without knowing SQL. They deploy to "the cloud" without understanding what a process is.

## a better approach

Start from the metal and work up. Teach memory management before garbage collection. Teach TCP before HTTP. Teach assembly before Python.
